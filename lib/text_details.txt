OpenDoors is a complete property booking and management platform designed for users and property owners.
 Users can discover apartments, hotels, short-let stays, and other accommodation types, make enquiries, complete bookings, and manage their stays directly from the app.
For property owners and managers, OpenDoors provides a robust admin dashboard featuring:
Property listing management
Image gallery and facility uploads
Booking workflow (waiting, confirmed, completed)
Enquiry tracking
Payment method setup
Earnings monitoring
Payout requests and settlement
User management
Gallery, package, and facility configuration
With integrated payment gateways, secure transactions, and real-time analytics, OpenDoors makes it easy to run and scale a property rental business.
 The platform offers a clean, user-friendly interface and supports both mobile app and web access.
OpenDoors aims to simplify accommodation booking and provide a seamless experience for both travelers and property managers.
